Welcome

Welcome to my visualisation about the F1.

The Formula one has evolved and changed a lot in the last years. The Pitstop strategy has changed from year to year. In some years almost every team drive the same strategy, in others it differs widely. While data about laptimes is available since 1996, data about pitstop times and frequency is only available since 2012.
